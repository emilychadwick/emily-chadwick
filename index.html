About Me

I am a front-end web developer and UX designer whose passion was sparked by my work outside of the tech industry.
As a non-profit manager, I've had to write, design, and edit emails, newsletters, and web pages for clients on tight budgets.
These tasks excited me more than my other projects, and I began to wonder why I wasn't building websites full time.
I've developed my coding skills, learned design tools and best practices, and am ready to dive right into exciting new challenges.
